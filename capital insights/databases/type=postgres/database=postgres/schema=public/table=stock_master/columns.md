# stock_master

**Dataset:** `public`

## Columns (9)

- symbol (string NOT NULL, "Symbol/Ticker of the company listed on National Stock Exchange in India.")
- company_name (string, "Name of the company.")
- isin (string, "ISIN of the company")
- categorization (string, "Categorization of the company into Large Cap, Mid Cap and Small Cap")
- market_capitalization (float64, "Latest market capitalisation of the company in Crores INR.")
- sector (string, "Sector of the company.")
- industry (string, "Industry of the company.")
- company_history (string, "Brief company history.")
- updated_on (timestamp(6), "Date on which data was updated")
