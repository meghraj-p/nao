# stock_pe_pb_peg

**Dataset:** `public`

## Columns (5)

- date (timestamp(6) NOT NULL, "Date in datetime format.")
- symbol (string NOT NULL, "Symbol or ticker of the company listed on the stock exchange.")
- pe (float64, "Price to Earnings Ratio (PE) of the company as on date in 'Date' column.")
- pb (float64, "Price to Book Value Ratio (PB) of the company as on date in 'Date' column.")
- peg (float64, "Price to Earnings Growth (PEG) Ratio of the company as on date in 'Date' column.")
